About Me

Welcome to my page! My name is Beth Spencer. I was born and raised in Southeast Kansas. I currently call Oswego home, with hopes of relocating to a bigger city in the near future. I'm a 31-year-old mother of two who loves all things music, exploring the outdoors, and spending time with my kids.
